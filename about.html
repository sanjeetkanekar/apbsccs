COMPREHENSIVE BENEFITS TO THE MEMBERS:
The Board of Directors is pleased to recommend the following benefits to the members of the Society as approved in the AGM:

  1. The Dividend is released for Ganesh Chaturthi Festival on obtaining for approval from AGM 15.00% Dividend is released for the year 2021-2022.
  2. The Retirement benefit of `. 5001/- are released in the AGM to those members who have retired from the Bank on attaining superannuation from the Bank & from the membership of the Society.
  3. The Society releases `. 5001/- to the family member of the deceased member of the Society on receipt of the claim / death certificate.
  4. The Educational Scholarship Scheme
    The Society acknowledges the excellence in the educational field of members & their family members by giving them scholarships. The Society releases the Educational Scholarship Scheme as per AGM approval to the children's of the member who secures 75% and above marks in 10th std., 12th Science, 12th Commerce, 12th Arts & 12th Vocational Stream.
