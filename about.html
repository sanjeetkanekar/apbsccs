During 1968, they have started the Society with Rs. 848/- i.e. Shares of Rs. 810/- and Entrance Fee of Rs. 38/-. The amount per member which was subscribed share was Rs.10/- & for Entrance Fee was Rs. 1/-. The achievements of the Society during these years were MRBS (Membership Retirement Benefit Scheme) & purchase of Flat during the tenure of Shri. P. S. Vernekar and mbda (members benefit death assurance) scheme during the tenure of Shri. Bhavanidas Manerikar. During our tenure we have modified the mbda scheme and enhanced loan limit up to Rs. 15.00 lakhs.

AT PRESENT COMPREHENSIVE BENEFITS TO THE MEMBERS:

  2. The Society releases `. 5001/- to the family member of the deceased member of the Society on receipt of the claim / death certificate.
