About Us

The Apex Bank’s Staff Co-operative Credit Society Ltd., C/o The Goa State Co-op. Bank ltd., “Sahakar Sankul”, EDC Complex, Patto Plaza, Panaji-Goa was registered on 24th February 1968 under the Registration Code No. RES-(a) 200/Goa and it has been classified as “Credit Resources Society”.


The membership of the society is open to any person who is a confirm staff/employee working in Goa State Co-operative Bank Ltd. The total membership strength of the society as on 31st March 2022 is 394 members.


Objectives of the Society:
  • To provide for social & economical betterments of its members through self help & mutual aid in accordance with the Co-operative principles
  • To promote economic & social development of its members either by itself or in co-operation with other organizations
  • To provide practical training or self help & co-operation to the members
  • To encourage the habit of saving among its members by mobilizing regular monthly Thrift / Cumulative deposit, Savings, Recurring, Fixed, MRBS & other types of deposits, including towards members Benefit death Assurance Fund
  • To offer retirement benefit & death relief services to its members.
  • To provide financial assistance in the form of loan to needy members for all reasonable purpose on reasonable terms & conditions
  • To act as an agent for joint purchase of the domestic & other requirement of its members on credit basis
  • To undertake any other activities which are conductive and necessary to attain its objects

The Society has achieved Good governance, greater heights and a committed management with the Board of Directors.


The Apex Bank’s Staff Co-operative Credit Society Ltd is very thankful to the Chairman, Vice Chairman and Board of Directors of the Goa State Co- op. Bank Ltd. for their support and assistance.


The Apex Bank’s Staff Co-operative Credit Society Ltd is also thankful to the Assistant Registrar of Central Zone and their staff members for their valuable co-operation, guidance and assistance.


We are thankful to the staff members of The Goa State Co-operative Bank Ltd., HO Branch for their excellent services rendered to the Society.

Want to become a Member ?

Any person appointed in Goa State Co-op Bank Ltd on Regular basis, can avail membership of the society in a prescribed form by subscribing Entrance fee of Rs.10/- and at least one fully paid up share of Rs.300/-. Further he /she shall contribute to the society a minimum monthly deposit of Rs.300/- till he continues to be a member of the society however, the General Body may increase and fixed the limit of monthly deposit from time to time.


Documents Required for Membership:
  • He/she should submit a duly filled prescribed application form of the society.
  • He/she should pay an entrance fee of Rs.10/- which is non refundable
  • He/she should pay one share of Rs. 300/- of the society along with the application form.
  • He/she gives in writing an irrevocable authority to drawing and disbursing officer of the department/establishment to recover by deduction from his/her salary, dues payable by him / her to the society.
